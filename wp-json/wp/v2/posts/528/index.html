Carolyn Potter shows crochet nudibranchs, polymer clay and gourd sculptures and fiber art, including small wearable pieces. Carolyn has been making amazing fiber art (and more) since the early 1970s. This is a wonderful opportunity to see work by an incredible artist who exhibits rarely.<\/p>\n
