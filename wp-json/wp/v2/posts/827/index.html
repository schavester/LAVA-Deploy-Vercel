Inhale\u00e2\u20ac\u00a6Exhale\u00e2\u20ac\u00a6For more than a century, Los Angeles\u00e2\u20ac\u2122 Barlow Respiratory Hospital has guided its patients and California to \u00e2\u20ac\u0153breathe easier.\u00e2\u20ac\u009d Opening on October 25th, Heidi Duckler Dance Theatre\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s (HDDT) Catch Your Breath will examine the intersection of art and learning through the fundamental act of breathing inspired by Barlow\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s 110-year heritage. Designed as an artistic journey through the environs of the hospital property, the performance will guide the audience through scenes motivated by the preserved and reinvigorated spaces of the Barlow facility, the history of the hospital, and specific patients\u00e2\u20ac\u2122 stories to recovery. Inspired by the individuals whose lives have been changed as a result of their time at Barlow, HDDT will highlight the hospital\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s past, while celebrating its future through captivating live performance. Tickets on sale now: www.heididuckler.org<\/p>\n
